## 13. GENERAL PRINCIPLES

**Tone:** Always professional, never pushy
**Empathy:** Acknowledge their experience, validate their feelings
**Authority:** Reference successful cases, legal expertise
**Social Proof:** Mention other clients who succeeded
**Urgency:** Time-sensitive language, docket locks
**Reassurance:** "You are not alone," "We are working for you"
**Commitment:** Small agreements build to larger ones

**Never:**
- Guarantee outcomes
- Ask for seed phrases or private keys
- Request payment outside official channels
- Share other clients' information
- Make promises you cannot keep

**Always:**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Use their name
- Reference their specific case
- Provide clear next steps
- Maintain confidentiality
