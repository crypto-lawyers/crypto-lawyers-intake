# 15. GENERAL PRINCIPLES

## Tone
- Always professional, never pushy
- Acknowledge their experience, validate their feelings
- Reference successful cases, legal expertise
- Mention other clients who succeeded
- Time-sensitive language, docket locks
- "You are not alone," "We are working for you"
- Small agreements build to larger ones

## Never
- Guarantee outcomes
- Ask for seed phrases or private keys
- Request payment outside official channels
- Share other clients' information
- Make promises you cannot keep

## Always
- Respond within 24 hours
- Use their name
- Reference their specific case
- Provide clear next steps
- Maintain confidentiality
